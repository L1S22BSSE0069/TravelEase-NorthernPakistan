How to Travel

  • Buses from major cities to Gilgit/Skardu
  • 4x4 Jeeps for local travel in valleys
  • Guided tours available at hotels

Where to Stay

  • Budget guesthouses in Hunza, Skardu
  • Hotels with heating in colder areas
  • Camp sites near Fairy Meadows

Budgeting Tips

  • Plan for Rs. 2,000–5,000 per day
  • Travel in groups to reduce transport costs
  • Eat local for authentic and cheaper meals

Packing Essentials

  • Warm clothes, trekking shoes
  • Water bottle, snacks
  • Power bank and mobile data
