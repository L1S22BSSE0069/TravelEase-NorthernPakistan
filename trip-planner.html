Trip Planning Guide for Northern Pakistan

How to Travel

  • Buses from Lahore/Islamabad to Gilgit, Hunza
  • Jeeps for Fairy Meadows, Skardu, etc.
  • Local guides available at destinations

Where to Stay

  • Budget: Hostels, Guest Houses
  • Mid-range: Hotels

Budgeting Tips

Bring cash — ATMs may be limited. Estimate ₹5,000–15,000 for a 3-day trip.

Packing Essentials

  • Warm clothes, hiking boots
  • Power banks, torch, sunscreen

Downloadable Resources

  • Packing Checklist
  • Basic Urdu Phrases

FAQs

  • Is Wi-Fi available? — Yes, in cities; limited in remote areas.
  • Do I need a guide? — Recommended for trekking routes.
