Explore Northern Pakistan: Trip Planner

Your guide to a well-prepared journey!

Transportation Options

  • Bus: Available from major cities to towns like Gilgit, Hunza, and Skardu.
  • Jeep: Essential for off-road and mountainous routes.
  • Local Guides: Hire guides for trekking, sightseeing, and language assistance.

Where to Stay

  • Hotels: Ranging from budget to luxury in major towns.
  • Guesthouses: Affordable and culturally immersive experiences in villages.

Budgeting Tips

  • Use public transport to save costs.
  • Eat at local dhabas for authentic and inexpensive food.
  • Group travel lowers accommodation and guide costs.

Packing Essentials

  • Warm clothing (layers, jacket, gloves)
  • Hiking boots and rain gear
  • First aid kit and personal medicines
  • Power bank and flashlight

FAQ

Is Wi-Fi available?
Yes, in most hotels and towns. Expect limited access in remote areas.
Do I need a guide?
Recommended for trekking and remote valleys. Guides ensure safety and local insight.
